Constructions

Construction is our experience

Planning and Design

Identify the project's purpose and requirements. Develop initial project plans and sketches. Create detailed architectural and engineering designs.

Site Preparation

Clear the construction site of any obstructions. Perform soil tests to ensure foundation stability. Excavate and grade the site to the required levels.

Foundation Construction

Dig and pour the building's foundation. Install footings, piers, and retaining walls. Lay the groundwork for utility lines like water, gas, and electricity.

Structural Framework

Erect the main support structure (steel, wood, or concrete framing). Install beams, columns, and load-bearing walls. Construct floors, ceilings, and roof structures.

Building Envelope

Install exterior walls, insulation, and sheathing. Set up windows, doors, and exterior finishes. Apply roofing materials to ensure weatherproofing. Build interior walls, partitions, and ceilings.

Final Touches and Inspection

Complete any remaining work like landscaping and exterior amenities. Conduct thorough inspections to ensure compliance with building codes.
